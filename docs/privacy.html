Privacy Policy — Fil-Grama

Last updated: June 2026

Fil-Grama ("the App") is an internal analytics tool used by a marketing agency to measure and report the organic performance of the social media accounts it manages on behalf of its clients.

Data we access

When an account owner authorizes the App through TikTok's official login screen, the App accesses, via TikTok's official API and only with that authorization: basic profile information (display name, profile link, bio, verified status); account statistics (follower, following, likes and video counts); and the list of the account's public videos and their public metrics. The App never accesses or stores TikTok passwords.

How we use the data

Solely to display analytics dashboards and generate performance reports for the account owner and the agency managing the account. Not used for advertising or unrelated purposes.

Storage and security

Stored on secured infrastructure; access tokens encrypted at rest. Retained only as long as needed to provide the service.

Sharing

We do not sell the data and do not share it with third parties.

Revocation and deletion

You can revoke access at any time from your TikTok settings. On revocation or request, the associated data is deleted.
